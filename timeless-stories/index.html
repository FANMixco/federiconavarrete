Federico Navarrete

is a Salvadoran Innovator and traveler who is on the quest to create a better world through technology. Also, he wants to recreate the Salvadoran perspective through folklore.

His professional career has been around IT. However, as an immigrant living in Poland and Spain, he discovered all the negative biases surrounding his homeland, El Salvador. The main reason was the lack of available information in other languages like English, Czech, German, Polish, etc. This situation motivated him to create the first series of Salvadoran folkloric books in English.

Each book contains 31 stories that have been shared orally for hundreds of years like Sir Francis Drake arriving with his Golden Hind and hiding a small treasure in Meanguera Island, and the twins whom a Shaman transformed into the Cadejos because of their antics.

As a person, he believes that everyone should be their own heroes. He shares with everyone he meets that the only person who can reshape their destiny is the one they see in the mirror daily. Dare to be your own hero TODAY.
